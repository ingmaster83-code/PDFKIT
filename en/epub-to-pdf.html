Frequently Asked Questions

Why does a print dialog appear?

EPUB to PDF conversion uses your browser's print feature. In the dialog, select 'Save as PDF' as the destination to download the PDF file.

Are images in the EPUB included?

Yes. Images embedded in the EPUB are included in the preview and the printed PDF.

Are files uploaded to a server?

No. All processing happens entirely within your browser — your files are never uploaded to any server. This zero-server architecture keeps your data 100% private, safe for sensitive documents and enterprise security environments.
