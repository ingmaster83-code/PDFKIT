Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Which PDFs give the best results?

Text-based PDFs with clearly defined tables work best. Scanned image PDFs without a text layer are not supported.

Q. How do I open the CSV in Excel?

Download the CSV file and open it in Excel. If Korean characters appear garbled, select UTF-8 BOM encoding and re-convert.

Q. What happens with multi-page PDFs?

Data from all pages is combined in order. Page separators are included to help you identify where each page starts.
