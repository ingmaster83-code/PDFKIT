Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Can I add only a header or only a footer?

Yes. Just fill in the header or footer field — whichever you leave blank will not be added.

Q. Can I apply it to specific pages only?

Currently, header and footer are applied to all pages. Per-page targeting is planned for a future update.

Q. Are my files sent to a server?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files are never uploaded anywhere.
