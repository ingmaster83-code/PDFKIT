Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What metadata fields can I edit?

You can edit the title, author, subject, keywords, creator, and producer fields of the PDF.

Q. Will editing metadata affect the PDF content?

No. Only the document properties are changed. The PDF content and formatting remain untouched.

Q. Are my files sent to a server?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files are never uploaded anywhere.
