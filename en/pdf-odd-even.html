Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Where is splitting by odd/even pages useful?

It is useful for duplex printing when your printer does not support it, or for organizing scanned pages from a double-sided document.

Yes. The original file is untouched and a new PDF is created for download.

Q. Can I download both odd and even pages at once?
