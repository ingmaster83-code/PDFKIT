Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How does the PDF comparison work?

Text is extracted from each PDF, then compared line by line. Added text is highlighted in green, deleted text in red.

Q. Can I compare scanned image PDFs?

Scanned PDFs without a text layer cannot be compared. This tool works best with text-based PDFs.

Q. Are my files uploaded to a server?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server.
