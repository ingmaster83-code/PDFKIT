Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from the Watermark tool?

Watermark automatically repeats the same text across every page. This editor lets you place text, shapes, and images one at a time, anywhere you choose, on any page — the two tools serve different purposes.

Can I edit the existing text in the PDF directly?

Not yet. You can add new text on top of the page or cover existing content with a whiteout box. Direct editing of the original text is planned for a future update.

Is my file uploaded to a server?

No. All processing happens entirely in your browser, and your file is never sent to any server.
