Q. Which page sizes are supported?

A3, A4, A5, Letter (US), and Legal (US) are supported.

Q. Will the content be scaled automatically?

Yes. The content on each page is automatically scaled to fit the new page size.

Q. Are my files sent to a server?

No. All processing is done locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server.
