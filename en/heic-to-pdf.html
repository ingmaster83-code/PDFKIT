Frequently Asked Questions

Can I merge multiple HEIC files into one PDF?

Yes. Select multiple HEIC files and they will be combined into a single PDF, one photo per page.

Is photo quality preserved?

Photos are converted to high-quality JPEG before being embedded in the PDF. The quality difference is barely noticeable.

Are files uploaded to a server?

No. All processing happens entirely in your browser. Your files are never sent to any server.
