Privacy Policy

✅ Effective: January 1, 2024  |  Last updated: January 1, 2024

WooaPDF ("we" or "the Service") takes your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use our service and how it is handled.

1. Information We Collect

1-1. File Data (Never Sent to Server)

All files processed by WooaPDF are handled entirely within your browser (computer). No PDF, image, or other file you upload is ever sent to or stored on our servers. All data is immediately discarded when processing is complete or the browser is closed.

1-2. Automatically Collected Information

The following information may be collected automatically for service improvement and analytics:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and access time
  • Operating system information
  • Referral source (search terms, referring site)

This information is not used to identify individuals and is used only for statistical analysis to improve the service.

2. Cookies & Advertising

2-1. Cookies

We may use cookies to improve your experience with the service. Cookies store user preferences (e.g. recently used options) and can be disabled in your browser settings.

2-2. Google AdSense

We may display ads through Google AdSense to cover the cost of running the service. Google may use cookies to show ads relevant to your interests. For more information about Google's use of advertising cookies, see Google Ads Policy.

2-3. Google Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to analyze visitor statistics. Collected data is anonymized and not used to identify individuals.

3. Purpose of Data Use

Collected information is used only for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving the service
  • Statistical analysis of service usage
  • Troubleshooting technical issues
  • Service security and abuse prevention

4. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not share personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • With explicit user consent
  • As required by law or requested by law enforcement
  • When using external services necessary for operation (e.g. Google, limited to anonymized statistical data)

5. Privacy Protection Measures

  • All file processing happens only within your browser
  • Encrypted communication via HTTPS
  • We do not collect unnecessary personal information
  • Statistical data is anonymized before storage

6. Your Rights

You may exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Withdraw consent for data collection and use
  • Refuse cookies (via browser settings)
  • Submit inquiries or objections regarding data handling

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under 14 years of age. We do not intentionally collect personal information from children under 14.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in response to legal or service changes. Updates will be posted on this page. Significant changes will be announced within the service.
