💡 Tips

  • In Chrome, allow font access permission when prompted for more accurate Korean font rendering in the converted PDF.
  • The first conversion downloads a ~6 MB WASM module. Subsequent conversions are much faster thanks to browser caching.
  • On mobile, the initial module download may be slow. A Wi-Fi connection is recommended.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which HWP versions are supported?

HWP 5.0 and later versions are supported. Some older files (HWP 3.x and below) may have limited conversion support.

Will complex tables or equations convert correctly?

Basic text, images, and table structures are converted. However, complex equations, special objects, or macros may not render perfectly. Always review the output PDF before use.

Why do fonts look different in the PDF?

If the Korean fonts used in the HWP file (e.g. Batang, Gulim) are not installed on your system, substitute fonts are used for rendering. In Chrome, allowing font access permission enables the tool to use your system fonts for more accurate results.

Are my files uploaded to a server?

No. All processing happens entirely within your browser — your files are never uploaded to any server. This zero-server architecture keeps your data 100% private, safe for sensitive documents and enterprise security environments.
